About Aster Consultants

Mumbai-based human resource placement firm delivering high-quality, customized, and research-driven talent solutions.

Our Story

Founded in 2021 by Tarikaa Rajput, Aster Consultants was established with a mission to redefine recruitment through a personalized, insight-led approach.

With deep expertise in the Banking, Financial Services, NBFC, MNC and Real Estate sectors, we specialize in recruitment across all levels — from Executive Leadership to Middle and Junior Management.

Our approach is strategic, consultative, and tailored to meet the unique hiring needs of every client. Whether you're scaling up, transforming your team, or seeking niche expertise, we ensure access to the best-fit talent that aligns with your business goals.

Our Mission

Empowering organizations with insight-led, high-quality recruitment solutions that drive growth and long-term success.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and respected talent partner in the BFSI and NBFC sectors — known for our strategic insight, service excellence, and ability to deliver transformative hiring solutions.

Meet the Founder – Tarikaa Rajput

Tarikaa Rajput is the visionary founder of Aster Consultants, established in 2021 with a mission to redefine recruitment through a personalized, insight-led approach.

With over 6 years of HR leadership experience at some of India's most respected financial institutions — including Reliance Mutual Fund, HDFC Bank, and JM Morgan Stanley — Tarikaa brings a deep understanding of the BFSI and NBFC landscape.

Her career has been rooted in strategic talent acquisition, leadership hiring, and organizational development, giving her the perspective and expertise to recognize the real drivers of business success: the right people in the right roles.
